Privacy Policy

How Rena Bean Creations collects, uses, and protects the information you share when you visit this site or place an order.

Last updated: June 2026

Rena Bean Creations ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us respond to you and fulfill your orders:

  • Information you provide. When you submit the contact or quote form, we collect the details you enter, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any project details or messages.
  • Order information. When you purchase a tee, your payment and shipping details are collected and processed by our checkout provider. We receive only the information needed to fulfill and ship your order.
  • Automatically collected information. Like most websites, basic technical information (such as browser type and general usage) may be collected through cookies and standard server logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes for quilting services.
  • To process, fulfill, and ship orders placed through the shop.
  • To communicate with you about your project or order.
  • To maintain, improve, and secure the website.

How Your Information Is Shared

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only with trusted service providers that help us operate, and only as needed to provide their service:

  • Form delivery. Contact and quote form submissions are delivered to us through a third-party form service.
  • Payments and checkout. Tee purchases are processed securely by our e-commerce and payment provider. We do not store your full card details.
  • Website hosting. This site is hosted on a third-party hosting platform.
  • Social media. If you contact us through Instagram, that interaction is governed by Instagram's own privacy policy.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies

This site uses a small number of cookies to support basic functionality and improve your experience. You can decline non-essential cookies using the banner shown on the site, and you can manage or clear cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Payments

All payments for shop items are handled by our secure checkout provider. Your card information is entered directly with that provider and is never stored on this website.

Data Retention

We keep the information you provide only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, complete your order, and meet ordinary record-keeping needs. You may ask us to remove your information at any time.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Choices

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you may opt out of any non-essential communications. To make a request, reach out through the contact page.

Third-Party Links

This site may link to other websites, such as Instagram or our checkout provider. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your information, please reach out through our contact page or message us on Instagram at @renabeancreations.
