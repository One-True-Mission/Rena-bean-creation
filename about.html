About Rena

Crafting Creativity
& Community Care

Meet Rena Hernandez, a talented quilt artist whose creative spirit weaves intricate stories through fabric. With a passion for quilting that goes far beyond a hobby, Rena has built a business around transforming ordinary quilt tops into extraordinary masterpieces that capture hearts and stand the test of time.

Based in Brock, Texas, Rena operates a longarm quilting studio where clients from across the country ship their quilt tops to be finished with professional care. Her reputation for fast turnaround, beautiful results, and genuine attention to detail has earned her a loyal following of quilters who trust her completely, often choosing "Quilter's Choice" and knowing they will never be disappointed.

What sets Rena apart is not just her technical skill, but her eye for color and design. Every quilt she touches is treated as a unique creative project. She considers the fabric, the story behind it, and the person it is being made for. The result is always something that feels intentional, personal, and made with genuine care.

When your quilt arrives back at your door, trimmed and ready to bind, you will understand why her clients keep coming back year after year.
