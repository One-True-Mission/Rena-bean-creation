Simple, Honest Pricing

Longarm quilting is priced by size and is some of the most affordable around, with most quilts finished in about two weeks. Here is exactly how the cost is figured so there are never any surprises.

How Pricing Works

Longarm quilting is just $0.15 per square inch. Measure your quilt's width and length in inches, multiply them together to get the total square inches, then multiply by $0.15.

Example 50 in wide × 80 in long = 4,000 sq in
4,000 × $0.15 = $60.00
  • Add-ons (binding, etc.)$25 / hour
  • Batting$12 / yard
  • TurnaroundAbout 2 weeks

How It Works

Simple Process

Sending your quilt across the country can feel daunting. Here is exactly what working with Rena looks like from start to finish.

01
Get Started

Reach Out

Fill out the contact form or send a message. Tell Rena about your quilt top, your fabric, and what you are envisioning. She will respond quickly with a plan and a price.

02
Ship It

Mail Your Quilt

Pack your quilt top and backing and ship it to Weatherford, Texas. USPS small prepaid boxes work perfectly and keep shipping affordable. Rena will confirm when it arrives.

03
We Create

Rena Gets to Work

Your quilt is finished with expert longarm craftsmanship. Every stitch placed with intention. When complete it is trimmed and ready to bind so it arrives ready to use.

04
Done

Shipped Back to You

Your finished quilt is carefully packed and shipped back. Clients across the country keep coming back because the result speaks for itself every single time.

Mail-In & Drop-Off

Getting Your Quilt to Rena

Whether you are around the corner or across the country, sending your quilt in is simple. Choose whichever option is easiest for you.

01
Step One

Package It Up

Fold your quilt and place it in a sturdy box or a padded shipping bag. A large zip bag inside adds a little extra protection from moisture in transit.

02
Step Two

Drop Off or Ship

Bring it to the Weatherford drop-off location below, or have any carrier (USPS, UPS, or FedEx) deliver it straight there. Both options work perfectly.

03
Step Three

Rena Takes It From Here

Once your quilt arrives, Rena confirms she has it and gets started. You will always know exactly where your quilt is in the process.

📍

Weatherford Drop-Off & Receiving
