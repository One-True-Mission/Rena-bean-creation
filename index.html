How It Works

Simple
Process

Sending your quilt across the country can feel daunting. Here is exactly what working with Rena looks like from start to finish.

01
Get Started

Reach Out

Fill out the contact form or send a message. Tell Rena about your quilt top, your fabric, and what you are envisioning. She will respond quickly with a plan and a price.

02
Ship It

Mail Your Quilt

Pack your quilt top and backing and ship it to Brock, Texas. USPS small prepaid boxes work perfectly and keep shipping affordable. Rena will confirm when it arrives.

03
We Create

Rena Gets to Work

Your quilt is finished with expert longarm craftsmanship. Every stitch placed with intention. When complete it is trimmed and ready to bind so it arrives in your hands ready to use.

04
Done

Shipped Back to You

Your finished quilt is carefully packed and shipped back. Clients across the country keep coming back because the result speaks for itself every single time.

About Rena

Crafting Creativity
& Community Care

Meet Rena Hernandez, a passionate quilt artist based in Brock, Texas, whose creative spirit weaves intricate stories through every piece of fabric she touches.

With a sharp eye for color, design, and detail, Rena transforms ordinary quilt tops into extraordinary masterpieces. Her clients ship from across the country and keep coming back because the result is simply better than anything they can find locally.

When you trust Rena with your fabric, you are trusting someone who treats every quilt like it is her own.
